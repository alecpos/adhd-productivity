## Introduction

Welcome to the ADHD Calendar system's new transparency and fairness features! This guide explains how our AI assistant works with you to provide fair, transparent, and ethical scheduling assistance while respecting your autonomy.

## What's New in This Update?

The latest update to your ADHD Calendar introduces several important improvements:

1. **Transparent Explanations**: Your calendar now explains why it makes specific recommendations
2. **Fairness Improvements**: Better recommendations for all users regardless of background
3. **Enhanced User Control**: More options to control when and how AI makes decisions
4. **Bias Protection**: Ongoing monitoring to ensure the system treats everyone fairly

## Understanding AI Explanations

### What are AI Explanations?

Whenever your ADHD Calendar suggests a schedule adjustment, recommends a task time, or provides an attention aid, you can now see **why** it made that recommendation. 

### How to Access Explanations

1. Look for the **"Why this recommendation?"** link beneath any AI suggestion
2. Click to expand a plain-language explanation
3. For more detail, select **"Show me more"** to see a visual breakdown of factors

### Example Explanations

Here are some examples of what you might see:

#### For a Schedule Recommendation:
> "This task was scheduled at 10:00 AM because:
> - Your productivity data shows you typically complete similar tasks efficiently at this time
> - You have a 30-minute buffer before your next meeting
> - This task has a medium attention demand that matches your typical morning energy level"

#### For a Duration Estimate:
> "This task is estimated to take 45 minutes because:
> - Your historical data with similar writing tasks shows an average of 42 minutes
> - The complexity level of this task is medium based on its description
> - You're scheduling it during a high-focus time of day, which typically improves your speed"

#### For an Attention Management Suggestion:
> "A break is recommended now because:
> - You've been working for 90 minutes without a pause
> - Your recent task switching pattern indicates potential attention fatigue
> - Similar breaks at this point have previously improved your afternoon focus"

## Fairness Features

### How We Ensure Fairness

The ADHD Calendar system is designed to work equally well for people of different:
- Neurotypes (ADHD subtypes, neurotypical, other neurodivergent profiles)
- Gender identities
- Age groups
- Educational backgrounds
- Cultural and language backgrounds

### What This Means For You

- Recommendations based on your individual patterns, not stereotypes
- Regular system checks to ensure different user groups receive equally helpful suggestions
- Ongoing improvements to reduce any detected biases

## Enhanced User Control

### Confirmation Options

You can now choose when the system should ask for your confirmation:
1. Navigate to **Settings > AI Assistant > Confirmation Preferences**
2. Choose from options like:
   - **Always confirm**: Ask before applying any AI recommendation
   - **Confirm important changes**: Only ask for significant schedule adjustments
   - **Confirm low-confidence suggestions**: Only ask when the AI is uncertain
   - **Rarely confirm**: Apply most recommendations automatically

### When AI Is Unsure

Sometimes, the AI system may be uncertain about a recommendation. When this happens:
1. You'll see a message like: **"I'm not completely confident about this suggestion."**
2. The system will explain why it's uncertain
3. You'll be given clear alternatives to choose from
4. Your choice helps the system learn your preferences for the future

### Fallback Options

If the AI system encounters a situation it doesn't understand:
1. It will clearly explain that it doesn't have enough information
2. It will offer you simple alternatives or defaults
3. You maintain complete control over the decision

## Privacy and Your Data

### Protected Attributes

To ensure fairness, the system may use demographic information such as:
- Age group
- General location (not precise)
- Neurotype profile

This information is:
- **Always optional** to provide
- **Securely encrypted**
- **Never shared** with third parties
- **Used only** to ensure fair treatment of all users

### Controlling Your Data

You can manage your fairness-related data at any time:
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy > My Profile**
2. Review what optional information you've provided
3. Update or remove any information
4. Choose whether to participate in fairness improvement

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will explanations slow down my experience?
No, explanations are generated in the background and available when you want them, but don't interrupt your workflow.

### Can I disable these features?
Yes, you can adjust or disable most fairness and explanation features in Settings. However, basic fairness protections remain active.

### How does the system know if it's being fair?
We conduct regular audits using statistical tests to ensure different user groups receive equally helpful recommendations.

### Will the system work well for my specific type of ADHD?
Yes! The system adapts to your individual patterns rather than using one-size-fits-all recommendations.

### Does the explanation system reveal my personal data?
No. Explanations are personalized but don't display your raw data. They focus on patterns and factors without exposing sensitive details.

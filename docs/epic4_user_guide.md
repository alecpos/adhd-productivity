## Overview

The Dynamic Schedule Rebalancing feature helps you optimize your daily schedule by aligning tasks with your natural energy patterns. It uses advanced machine learning to understand your personal circadian rhythm and cognitive patterns, then schedules tasks when you're most likely to perform them effectively.

### Key Benefits

- **Energy-Optimized Scheduling**: Automatically schedule high-focus tasks during your peak energy hours
- **Personalized Adaptation**: Learns your unique patterns over time for increasingly accurate scheduling
- **Cognitive Load Balancing**: Distributes tasks throughout the day based on their mental demands
- **Dynamic Rescheduling**: Intelligently adjusts your schedule when plans change or tasks are incomplete
- **Improved Task Completion**: Increases likelihood of completing tasks by scheduling them at optimal times

## Getting Started

### Initial Setup

1. Navigate to **Settings > Scheduling Preferences**
2. Enable the **Dynamic Schedule Rebalancing** feature
3. Complete the initial **Energy Pattern Questionnaire** to provide baseline data
4. Allow at least 2 weeks of app usage for the system to learn your patterns

### Quick Start Guide

1. Add tasks to your task list with proper metadata (duration, cognitive demands, flexibility)
2. Click **Optimize Schedule** from the calendar view
3. Review the suggested optimized schedule
4. Apply the optimization or make manual adjustments
5. Provide feedback on task completion to improve future optimizations

## Core Features

### Circadian Rhythm Detection

The system tracks patterns in your productivity, focus levels, and energy to determine your unique circadian rhythm pattern.

**How it works**:
- Analyzes when you complete tasks most effectively
- Considers your reported energy levels throughout the day
- Integrates with sleep data (if available)
- Builds a personalized energy curve that predicts your focus levels by hour

### Task Cognitive Profiling

Tasks are automatically categorized based on their cognitive demands to match them with appropriate energy states.

**Categories include**:
- **Focus-Intensive**: Tasks requiring deep concentration and attention
- **Creative**: Tasks benefiting from divergent thinking and creativity
- **Administrative**: Routine tasks with lower cognitive demands
- **Decision-Making**: Tasks requiring executive function and judgment

### Dynamic Rescheduling

When your day doesn't go as planned, the system can intelligently reschedule tasks:

**Triggers for rescheduling**:
- Incomplete tasks from previous days
- New high-priority tasks added to your schedule
- Extended duration of completed tasks
- Schedule conflicts or interruptions

## Settings and Preferences

### Circadian Preferences

Customize how the system interprets and applies your circadian patterns:

- **Manual Override**: Set known peak focus hours if you're already aware of your patterns
- **Weekend Differentiation**: Specify different patterns for weekdays vs. weekends
- **Sensitivity**: Adjust how aggressively the system optimizes based on energy levels

### Task Type Weighting

Control how different factors influence task scheduling:

- **Priority Weighting**: How much task priority overrides energy optimization
- **Deadline Pressure**: How much approaching deadlines influence scheduling
- **Cognitive Matching**: Strength of matching task types to energy states

### Learning Preferences

Manage how the system learns and adapts:

- **Adaptation Speed**: How quickly the system adjusts to new patterns
- **Data Sources**: Select which data points to include in pattern detection
- **Reset Learning**: Option to reset the system's learned patterns

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue**: Schedule optimization seems random or counterintuitive
- **Solution**: Ensure you've been using the app for at least 2 weeks and providing regular feedback
- **Solution**: Check if task metadata (focus required, etc.) is accurately set

**Issue**: Energy predictions don't match your actual experience
- **Solution**: Use the manual energy reporting feature for 7-10 days to recalibrate
- **Solution**: Check if major lifestyle changes (new job schedule, travel) are affecting predictions

**Issue**: Optimized schedule is too rigid
- **Solution**: Adjust flexibility settings in Scheduling Preferences
- **Solution**: Mark more tasks as "flexible" in task details

### Diagnostic Tools

Access these tools under **Settings > System > Diagnostics**:

1. **Pattern Visualization**: View your detected circadian patterns
2. **Optimization Explanation**: See why tasks were scheduled at specific times
3. **Learning Progress**: Track how the system is learning from your data
4. **Data Quality Check**: Identify if you need more data for better predictions

## FAQs

**Q: How long does it take for the system to learn my patterns?**
A: The system begins providing basic optimizations immediately, but it typically takes 2-3 weeks to develop a personalized energy model. Accuracy continues to improve over time.

**Q: Can I have different schedules for different days?**
A: Yes, the system automatically detects different patterns for weekdays vs. weekends. You can also manually set different preferences for specific days.

**Q: How does the system handle irregular sleep schedules?**
A: The system adapts to detect multiple potential patterns. For best results, use the sleep tracking integration or manually log significant sleep changes.

**Q: Will the system work if I have irregular work hours?**
A: Yes, the system learns your patterns regardless of when they occur. It doesn't assume a 9-5 schedule.

**Q: How can I manually override the optimization?**
A: You can always drag and drop tasks after optimization, lock specific tasks in place before optimization, or adjust the optimization strength in settings.

## Tips and Best Practices

### Getting the Most Accurate Predictions

1. **Consistent Feedback**: Rate your energy levels at least 3 times per day
2. **Detailed Task Metadata**: Set accurate cognitive demands for each task
3. **Completion Tracking**: Faithfully track when tasks are actually completed
4. **Sleep Tracking**: Connect sleep data for more accurate predictions

### Optimizing Your Workflow

1. **Task Batching**: Create related tasks for similar work to enable batching
2. **Energy Tagging**: Tag tasks with energy requirements (High, Medium, Low)
3. **Flexibility Settings**: Mark truly flexible tasks to give the optimizer more freedom
4. **Regular Review**: Weekly review your optimization success and adjust preferences

### Integrating with Other Workflows

1. **Calendar Blocking**: Use optimization before blocking time on shared calendars
2. **Meeting Scheduling**: Check optimized schedule before setting meeting times
3. **Deadline Management**: Set internal deadlines based on predicted energy patterns
4. **Project Planning**: Use energy forecasts when estimating project timelines

## Glossary

**Circadian Rhythm**: Your body's natural ~24-hour cycle of energy levels, alertness, and focus capacity

**Cognitive Demand**: The type and amount of mental effort required to complete a task

**Energy Curve**: A visualization of your predicted energy and focus levels throughout the day

**Optimization Strength**: How aggressively the system will rearrange tasks based on energy patterns

**Task Flexibility**: How much a task can be moved to different times without negative consequences

**Temporal Pattern**: Recurring patterns in productivity, focus, or task completion over time

**Rebalancing**: The process of rearranging tasks to better align with energy patterns or to accommodate changes
